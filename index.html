Where we dive

Las Catalinas & its ecosystem

A cluster of volcanic islands off Tamarindo, one of Costa Rica's richest dive playgrounds.

Rising from the Pacific just north of Tamarindo, the Catalina Islands channel cool, nutrient-rich currents.

From December to May, giant manta rays glide through the islands, joined year-round by eagle rays, reef sharks, turtles and huge schools of fish.
