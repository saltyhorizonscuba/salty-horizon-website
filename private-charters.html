Whale season · Jul–Nov

Whale season, from the water

From July to November, humpback whales migrate along this stretch of the Guanacaste coast to breed and calve, one of the longest migrations recorded for the species. From the flybridge of Playgrounds you get a front-row seat above the water, with the chance to slip in for a snorkel alongside dolphins riding the bow wave. Sightings are never guaranteed, wild animals keep their own schedule, but this is the season to be out there looking.

In partnership with Playgrounds

A 51-foot playground, all to yourselves

Playgrounds is a private power catamaran based right here in Tamarindo: three cabins, three bathrooms, a wide aft cockpit and an open flybridge built for lounging in the sun. Captain Pete has navigated these waters for more than 15 years, and his crew takes care of everything on board, so the only decision left is where to point the bow.
