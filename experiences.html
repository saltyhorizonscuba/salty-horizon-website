Discover Scuba Diving

from $170

Discover scuba diving with the Discover Scuba Diving program, a beginner-friendly introduction with no prior certification required. After a briefing with your instructor, enjoy two dives of about 45 minutes to 1 hour to explore the seabed and observe the richness of Costa Rica’s marine life: tropical fish, sea turtles, rays, moray eels and, depending on the season, the majestic manta rays. An unforgettable first experience, in complete safety.

Fun Dive · Bat Islands

Contact us

The Bat Islands (Islas Murciélagos), off Santa Rosa National Park in Guanacaste, offer one of Costa Rica’s most legendary dives. Accessible from May to November, they let experienced divers observe the impressive bull sharks in their natural habitat. An intense experience in the heart of exceptional marine life.

  • 3 guided dives
  • Snacks & drinks included
  • All scuba equipment included
  • Photos & videos included
  • For Advanced Open Water divers only
